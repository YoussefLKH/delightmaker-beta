FAQ

Questions?
We've got answers.

Almost nothing. Once you've uploaded your team and set your gifting rules, we take it from there. You'll get a one-click approval email 7 days before each delivery — or turn on auto-approve and you won't even need to do that.

Just note their dietary needs when you upload your team — gluten-free, nut-free, vegan, kosher. We automatically route those orders to the appropriate specialist bakery. Every employee gets a treat that's safe for them.

We currently serve the Halifax Regional Municipality (HRM) — including Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, Sackville, and surrounding areas. Delivery is always free within HRM.

Yes. From the approval email or your dashboard, you can modify or cancel any upcoming order up to 48 hours before the delivery date. No charge for cancelled orders.

You pay per treat delivered. Pricing is included in each order — no monthly fees or subscriptions. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. All employee data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with PIPEDA — Canada's federal privacy law. We never sell or share your team's information. You can request full data deletion at any time.
